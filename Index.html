MATRIX
About it:


The Matrix (1999), directed by the Wachowski sisters,is a science fiction film that revolutionized cinema with

its blend of action, philosophy, and innovative visual effects The story follows Thomas Anderson, a computer programmer who leads

a double life as a hacker under the pseudonym "Neo." Anderson feel that something in his world doesn't fit, a feeling of emptiness and

doubt about what is real. His life changes when he meets Morpheus and Trinity, who reveal the truth: the reality he perceives is actually

the Matrix, a digital simulation created by intelligent machines. In the real world, humans are cultivated and maintained in a dream state to be used as a power source by the machines.
