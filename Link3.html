Character

🧑‍💻 Neo (Thomas A. Anderson)
Actor: Keanu Reeves
A hacker who discovers the truth about the Matrix.
Believed to be "The One", a savior destined
to free humanity.

🕶️ Morpheus:
Actor: Laurence Fishburne
Leader of the human resistance. He finds and
mentors Neo, believing he is The One.

🖤 Trinity:
Actor: Carrie-Anne Moss
A skilled fighter and hacker. She is part of
Morpheus's crew and
evelops a strong bond—and love—for Neo.

👔 Agent Smith:
Actor: Hugo Weaving
A powerful and intelligent program that
enforces the rules of the Matrix.
He sees humans as a virus and wants
to destroy Neo.

🧠 The Oracle:
Actor: Gloria Foster
A wise program who guides Neo and others
with cryptic advice. She helps Neo
understand his potential and fate.

🧢 Cypher (Mr. Reagan):
Actor: Joe Pantoliano
A member of Morpheus’s crew who betrays them,
wishing to return to the comfortable illusion
of the Matrix.
